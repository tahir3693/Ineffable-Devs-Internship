Interactive Embeds

Want to enable the ClipMine experience on your blog or Twitter feed? Just embed our Content-Aware Video Player. It is responsive and built to work across different modern browsers and devices. Found an interesting point in video? On ClipMine adding tags is not reserved for the video owners only - any community member can do it. In addition our smart algorithms can automatically suggest tags (in private bets) for a wide variety of videos!
